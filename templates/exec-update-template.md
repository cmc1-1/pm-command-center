## Formatting Guidelines for This Template
- Write for scanners, not readers — assume leadership will spend 60 seconds on this
- Use metrics and status indicators — they communicate faster than prose
- Lead every section with the most important item, not chronological order
- If something is fine and unremarkable, don't include it — only report on what's notable
- Never pad the update to make it look like more happened — concise > comprehensive
- End with what you need from them, or explicitly state you need nothing
