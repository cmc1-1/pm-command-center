## ID Convention
- Format: AI-[NNN] (e.g., AI-001, AI-042)
- Sequential, never reused
- Next available ID: AI-001

## Source Convention
Format: "[Meeting type] MM/DD" or "[Email] MM/DD" or "[Slack] MM/DD" or "[Manual] MM/DD"
Examples: "Stakeholder Review 04/15", "1:1 w/ Sarah 04/14", "Email from VP Eng 04/13"
