# Stakeholder Map

Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD

> **Purpose**: Map every stakeholder by power and interest with engagement strategy, known positions, and communication preferences.
> **When to use**: Week 1 of any new role or large initiative, then refreshed quarterly. Referenced by `email-drafter` and `exec-update` skills for altitude calibration.
> **Audience**: You (primary). Confidential — never shared.

---

## Power / Interest Grid

Map each stakeholder into one of four quadrants to calibrate your engagement strategy:

```
                        HIGH INTEREST
                             │
          KEEP SATISFIED     │     MANAGE CLOSELY
          (High power,       │     (High power,
           low interest)     │      high interest)
          Monitor for shifts │     Your key partners —
          in interest.       │     invest heavily here.
          Regular updates,   │     Frequent communication,
          no surprises.      │     collaborative decision-making.
                             │
  LOW POWER ─────────────────┼───────────────────── HIGH POWER
                             │
          MONITOR            │     KEEP INFORMED
          (Low power,        │     (Low power,
           low interest)     │      high interest)
          Minimal effort.    │     They care but can't
          Don't ignore, but  │     block you. Keep them
          don't over-invest. │     informed, leverage as
                             │     advocates.
                             │
                        LOW INTEREST
```
