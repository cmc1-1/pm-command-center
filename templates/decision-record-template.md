## How to Use This Log
Every product decision gets recorded here with its rationale, alternatives considered, and who decided. This creates institutional memory — when someone asks "why did we do X?", the answer is here. The Meeting Summarizer skill auto-appends decisions; you can also add them manually.

## Decision Categories (for filtering)

When logging decisions, tag with one or more categories:
- **scope**: Feature scope, what's in/out
- **timeline**: Deadlines, milestones, launch dates
- **technical**: Architecture, infrastructure, technology choices
- **design**: UX/UI decisions, design direction
- **resource**: Staffing, budget, contractor decisions
- **process**: How the team works, workflow changes
- **strategy**: Product direction, positioning, market focus
- **customer**: Customer-specific commitments or accommodations
