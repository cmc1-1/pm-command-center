## How to Use This Journal
Log every win, outcome, and meaningful contribution as it happens. Don't wait — context fades within hours. The Impact Journal skill auto-appends entries with tags; you can also add manually. This journal feeds directly into your promotion packet.

**Why this matters**: At review time, you'll have documented evidence across all 7 Principal PM competencies instead of trying to remember 6 months of work.

---

## Impact Taxonomy

Tag each entry with one or more categories:
- **SHIP**: Launched a feature, product, or capability
- **METRIC**: Moved a measurable business metric
- **INFLUENCE**: Changed a decision, unblocked a team, or shifted direction
- **STRATEGY**: Contributed strategic thinking that shaped roadmap or vision
- **PROCESS**: Improved a workflow, created a playbook, or reduced friction
- **MENTORSHIP**: Helped someone grow, shared expertise, or elevated a team member
- **CROSS-ORG**: Connected dots across teams, drove synergy, or enabled collaboration
- **INNOVATION**: Introduced a new idea, approach, or technology
- **TRUST**: Built or deepened a key relationship (customer, stakeholder, partner)
- **RESCUE**: Saved a project, resolved a crisis, or prevented a failure
